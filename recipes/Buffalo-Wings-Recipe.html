Franks Red Hot Buffalo Wings Recipe

Description

Franks Red Hot Buffalo Wings are a culinary masterpiece renowned for its mouthwatering flavor and tender texture. Slow-cooked to perfection, this dish features a robust and smoky essence that permeates every fiber of the meat. The crust, known as the bark, forms a flavorful and caramelized exterior, while the interior remains succulent and juicy. The seasoning, often a blend of spices including salt, pepper, and sometimes a secret rub, enhances the natural beefy richness, creating a harmonious balance of savory and subtly sweet undertones. As you savor each bite, the layers of smoke, spice, and slow-cooked goodness unfold, making Franks Red Hot Buffalo Wings an iconic and unforgettable culinary experience that captures the essence of Southern barbecue tradition. Whether enjoyed on its own or in a sandwich, the taste of Franks Red Hot Buffalo Wings is a celebration of skillful craftsmanship and the love for exceptional, smoky flavors.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Wings
  • Franks Red Hot Sauce
  • Butter
  • Garlic Powder
  • Onion Powder
  • Black Pepper
  • Kosher Salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
  2. Line a baking sheet with aluminum foil and lightly grease with cooking spray.
  3. Place chicken wings in a large bowl and pat dry with paper towels.
  4. Combine garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper in a small bowl. Sprinkle over chicken wings and toss to coat.
  5. Arrange chicken wings on the prepared baking sheet.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven until chicken is no longer pink in the center and juices run clear, 25 to 30 minutes.
  7. Combine hot sauce and butter in a small saucepan over medium heat. Cook and stir until butter is melted and mixture begins to boil, about 5 minutes.
  8. Transfer baked chicken wings to a large bowl. Pour sauce over wings and toss to coat.
