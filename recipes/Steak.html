Perfectly Cooked Steak Recipe

Description

Perfectly cooked steak is a culinary masterpiece renowned for its mouthwatering flavor and tender texture. Slow-cooked to perfection, this dish features a robust and smoky essence that permeates every fiber of the meat. The crust, known as the bark, forms a flavorful and caramelized exterior, while the interior remains succulent and juicy. The seasoning, often a blend of spices including salt, pepper, and sometimes a secret rub, enhances the natural beefy richness, creating a harmonious balance of savory and subtly sweet undertones. As you savor each bite, the layers of smoke, spice, and slow-cooked goodness unfold, making perfectly cooked steak an iconic and unforgettable culinary experience that captures the essence of Southern barbecue tradition. Whether enjoyed on its own or in a sandwich, the taste of perfectly cooked steak is a celebration of skillful craftsmanship and the love for exceptional, smoky flavors.

Ingredients

  • Steak
  • Black Pepper
  • Kosher Salt
  • Butter
  • Thyme
  • Garlic

Steps

  • Remove the steak from the fridge and let it come to room temperature.
  • Pat the steak dry with paper towels.
  • Season the steak with salt and pepper.
  • Heat the pan over high heat and add the oil.
  • Place the steak in the pan and cook for 2 minutes.
  • Flip the steak and cook for another 2 minutes.
  • Add the butter, thyme, and garlic to the pan.
  • Baste the steak with the butter.
  • Remove the steak from the pan and let it rest for 5 minutes.
  • Slice the steak and serve.
